## Docs frontmatter

Use this schema:

- `title` (required)
- `navTitle` (optional)
- `description` (optional)
- `section` and `sectionLabel` (optional)
- `order` and `sectionOrder` (optional)
- `updatedAt` (optional)
- `tags` (optional)
- `draft` (optional)

## Sorting behavior

Pages are ordered by section, then page order, then title.
