SMB owners do not have time to translate their business into a 20-page brief.
Convelt makes it a 12-question conversation.

## The flow

- Collect hours, services, and differentiators.
- Draft copy with Gemini.
- Pair with a trusted visual template.

The result is a site that feels like it was designed for a real business—not a
placeholder.
