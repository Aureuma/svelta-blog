Running a dental practice is already a full-time sprint. Your website should
reduce friction, not add to it.

## The Convelt flow

1. **Answer a few prompts.** Hours, services, insurance, and locations.
2. **Pick a visual direction.** Calm, confident, and clinical.
3. **Launch with clarity.** Online booking front and center.

## What we optimize for

- Fast patient access to booking
- Trust-building visuals and testimonials
- Local SEO ready structure

Convelt is built to keep dentists focused on patient care, not web maintenance.
