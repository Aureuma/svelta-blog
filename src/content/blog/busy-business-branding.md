Great branding doesn’t require a six-month engagement. It needs focus.

## The five decisions

- **Voice:** calm, bold, or inviting?
- **Palette:** one base color and one electric accent.
- **Proof:** reviews, awards, or local features.
- **Clarity:** the three services you want sold first.
- **Momentum:** a single action you want visitors to take.

Convelt bakes these decisions into the first draft so your site feels coherent
from day one.
