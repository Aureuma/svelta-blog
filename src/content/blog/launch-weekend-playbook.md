When time is short, the plan must be tight. Here’s how our teams ship by
Sunday.

## Saturday: Structure

- Confirm services, hours, and location.
- Upload logo and two hero images.
- Lock the call-to-action.

## Sunday: Polish

- Add testimonials and social proof.
- Review mobile layout.
- Ship and share the link.

Convelt’s templates are pre-wired for this cadence, so you spend time on the
message, not the markup.
